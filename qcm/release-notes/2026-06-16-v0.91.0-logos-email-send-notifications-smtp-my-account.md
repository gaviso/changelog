Cumulative release since 0.26.0.

**Highlights**
- **Client logos** — upload a square logo per client; colored-initial avatars (deterministic per client) elsewhere.
- **Send quote to client** — compose-and-confirm email modal: primary contact in To, secondary contact + "send me a copy" + extra addresses in Cc; From uses the SMTP sending address; sends are logged to quote activity.
- **Email notifications** — a new per-event notification channel alongside the reminder webhook and Slack.
- **SMTP delivery** — Settings → Email (SMTP): host/port/encryption/auth/from, with a "Save & send test" (15s connect timeout).
- **My account** — manage your own name/email/password from inside the plugin.
- **Branding** — Business identity card redesigned into a three-column layout (logo on the left).
- **Permissions** — "Land on QCM dashboard after login" toggle.
- **UI polish** — rounded button focus rings, navigation buttons no longer show the browser link-preview, full-width fields, removed stray divider lines, sidebar now shows the plugin version + copyright.

See CHANGELOG.md for the complete list.
