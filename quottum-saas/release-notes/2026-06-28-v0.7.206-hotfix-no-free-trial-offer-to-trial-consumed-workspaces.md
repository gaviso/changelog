Money-correctness hotfix.

**Bug:** A workspace that had already used its 14-day trial (`trial_used_at` set) was still shown "Start 14-day trial · no card required · you won't be charged during the trial" — but `resolveTrialDays()` correctly returns 0 for such a workspace, so Stripe Checkout charged the full amount immediately. The promise and the charge disagreed.

**Fix:** Threaded trial eligibility through every surface that makes the promise, sourced from one place:
- `upgrade-form-props.ts` now fetches `trial_used_at` and returns `trialEligible = !trial_used_at` (so the upgrade page *and* the inline billing accordion both get it).
- `UpgradeForm` — when not eligible: per-plan CTA is **"Subscribe"** (not "Start N-day trial"), the "free trial · no card required" headline is dropped, and the disclaimer reads "Your first charge runs at checkout."
- The Free-plan upsell card on the billing page no longer promises a trial once one's been used.

The UI now honestly matches the backend — no "no card required" on a flow that charges right away. No migration.
