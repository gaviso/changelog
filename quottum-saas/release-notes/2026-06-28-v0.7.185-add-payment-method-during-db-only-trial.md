Unblocks the "add card during free trial" UX on both billing surfaces. The previous flow assumed every paying workspace had a Stripe customer; signup workspaces on a DB-only trial (paid plan + \`plan_status='trialing'\` + null \`stripe_subscription_id\`) hit "No billing history yet — subscribe first." on every attempt to update payment.

### Added

- **\`<AddPaymentMethodForTrial>\`** on \`/dashboard/settings/billing\` — replaces the SetupIntent-only "Update payment method" button when no Stripe sub exists yet. Routes through \`/api/billing/checkout\` (creates customer + Stripe subscription with carryover trial via existing \`resolveTrialDays()\` logic), then redirects to Stripe Checkout with card collection required. Workspaces that already have a Stripe sub keep using the existing \`<PaymentMethodUpdate>\` modal.
- **\`force_card_collection\`** body flag on \`/api/billing/checkout\`. When true, flips Stripe's \`payment_method_collection\` from \`'if_required'\` to \`'always'\`. Signup-time checkout flow unchanged — the "no card required up front" promise still holds for fresh signups.

### Changed

- **\`/dashboard/upgrade\` reshapes for already-trialing workspaces.**
  - Title: **"Manage <name> plan"** instead of **"Upgrade"**.
  - Headline copy next to the cadence toggle becomes **"Your trial ends <DATE> · remaining days carry over"** instead of the generic **"14-day free trial · no card required"**.
  - **Current plan card** gets an accent border + "Current plan" eyebrow. Its CTA becomes **"Add payment method"** (with \`force_card_collection\`), matching the billing-page path.
  - **Other plans** show **"Switch to <Name>"** — server-side \`resolveTrialDays()\` carries the remaining trial onto the new plan.
  - **Bottom disclaimer** switches from the fresh-trial pitch to the carryover phrasing.
- **Yearly price reads \`plans.prices.year\`** (operator-managed via \`/admin/plans\`) instead of computing \`monthly × 12 × 0.80\`. Cents-level drift fix — yearly Pro now shows the exact \$278 the operator entered, not \$278.40 computed.

2. \`/dashboard/settings/billing\` → "Add payment method" → confirm Stripe Checkout opens with card required → enter test card → confirm return to billing page with Stripe sub now present.
3. \`/dashboard/upgrade\` → confirm title is "Manage", headline mentions trial-end date, current plan card shows "Add payment method", other plan shows "Switch to <Name>", yearly toggle shows \$278/\$758.
4. Sign in as a Free workspace → confirm \`/dashboard/upgrade\` unchanged ("Start 14-day trial" / "Subscribe" CTAs preserved).
