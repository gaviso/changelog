### Fixed

- **\`resolveTrialDays\` respects the target plan's catalog \`trial_days\`.** A Pro-trialing workspace switching to Business (Business.trial_days=0 per /admin/plans) was landing in Stripe Checkout with "14 days free, then \$79/mo on Jul 11" because the carryover branch fired before checking the target plan. Catalog is now precedence-1: target plan has no trial → 0 trial days, regardless of the workspace's current trial state on a different plan. Pro→Pro carryover (the actual "add card mid-trial" path) is unaffected.
- **Upgrade-page disclaimer copy** when \`isAlreadyTrialing\` was "remaining trial days carry over when you pick any plan" — accurate only for trial-eligible target plans. Now "Plans that include a trial carry your remaining days over. Plans without a trial begin charging at checkout."
